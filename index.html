Press "Start Game" to begin. Watch for the flashing colored button and press it. Each level adds another button to the sequence, which must be repeated in order. A delay between button presses prevents quick pressing and confusion. The top score is displayed in the top left. If you press the wrong button, you lose and must press "Start Game" to replay with a fresh score of 0 and a new random sequence. The top score remains.
